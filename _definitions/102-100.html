Let $$M = (Q, Σ, δ, q_0, F)$$ be a deterministic finite automaton (DFA). We define the extended transition function

$$$δ^* : Q × Σ^* → Q$$$

as follows:

  1. For every $$q ∈ Q$$, $$δ^∗(q, ε) = q$$
  2. For every $$q ∈ Q$$, every $$y ∈ Σ^*$$, and every $$a ∈ Σ$$,

$$$δ^∗(q, ya ) = δ(δ^∗(q, y), a )$$$
