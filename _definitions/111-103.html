Let $$M = (Q, Σ, δ, q_0, F)$$ be a nondeterministic finite automaton (ε-NFA) and $$S ⊆ Q$$. We define the ε-closure of $$S$$,

$$$E(S)$$$

as follows:

  1. $$S ⊆ E(S)$$
  2. For every $$q ∈ E(S)$$, $$δ(q, ε) ⊆ E(S)$$
